				Helm v2-to-v3 Workshop			






October 21, 2020Virtual Event#highway2helm3

Thank you for attending the Helm Workshop: v2 to v3!  We hope you found the Helm 3 migration best practices, tips, and tricks valuable.
PresentationsPresentation PDFs can be found by navigating to the specific talk on the Helm Workshop schedule; they are listed under the speaker bios.
VideosThe workshop session recordings will be available on the CNCF YouTube channel by October 30, 2020. 
As you work through the migration, additional resources are available:
Helm v2 Deprecation TimelineHelm 2 to 3 pluginMigrating Helm v2 to v3How to migrate from Helm v2 to Helm v3Helm DocsHelm Community Resources
Don’t miss the Helm maintainers at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America – register today!

As Helm v2 support comes to an end in November 2020, join the Helm maintainers for a free half-day workshop. We’ll be covering what’s in Helm 3, Helm 3 migration best practices, tips and tricks, and more!
