The RISC-V Forum: Embedded Technologies registration is complimentary to both members and non-members.

REGISTRATION CLOSED


Our registration system is now integrated with the Linux Foundation Single sign-on (SSO) platform. To register you will be prompted to log in with your existing SSO account or to create a new one.
If you have forgotten the password for an existing account, reset it by clicking “Forgot Password.”
You will also be able to use 3rd party authentication providers (Gmail, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Facebook) to automatically generate your SSO Account.
If you experience issues, click the “Contact Us” link on the SSO page to connect with our support team.

INVOICES & CERTIFICATES OF ATTENDANCE
Registration InvoicesYou should receive an invoice upon registration, however, if you need an additional copy or an updated invoice please submit a request here. 
Certificates of AttendanceTo request a Certificate of Attendance, please submit a request here. Please Note: We verify attendance through the registration system, and Certificate of Attendance letters are sent out after the event is completed. 

CANCELLATIONS & SUBSTITUTIONS
So that we can better prepare for the event, if you are unable to attend, please log back into your account and select “Un-RSVP” or email us at events@risv.org.
