						Register					





You will have the ability to collaborate with the RISC-V community – to share best practices and innovate together. You can expect to network with other attendees, attend presentations with live Q&A, interact with sponsors in real-time, and much more!
Attendee Registration: $50 USD
Speakers: You should have received a registration link in your acceptance email. If you did not, please contact events@riscv.org for more details.

REGISTRATION CLOSED


Our registration system is now integrated with the Linux Foundation Single sign-on (SSO) platform. After clicking “Register Now”, you will be prompted to log in with your existing SSO account or to create a new one.
If you have forgotten the password for an existing account, reset it by clicking “Forgot Password.”
You will also be able to use 3rd party authentication providers (Gmail, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Facebook) to automatically generate your SSO Account.
If you experience issues, click the “Contact Us” link on the SSO page to connect with our support team.

CANCELLATIONS & SUBSTITUTIONS
So that we can better prepare for the event, if you are unable to attend, please log back into your registration using your confirmation number and select “unregister” or email us at events@risv.org.
