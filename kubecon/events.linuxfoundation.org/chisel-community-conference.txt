				Third Chisel Community Conference			








January 29 – 30, 2020
Western Digital
Milpitas, CA

On January 29 – 30, CHIPS Alliance will host CCC2020, the 3rd Chisel Community Conference. The first day will have a full day of presentations on Chisel topics, followed by a poster session. The second day will be a Chisel working day with break out rooms.
