						LF Training Discounts					





Linux Foundation Training is pleased to offer discounts for each attendee ticket type for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America Virtual. Each coupon is only available for attendees of KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America Virtual and will expire on 08:00 UTC on November 21, 2020. They cannot be combined with any other discount or promotion. Only valid for net new training purchases and cannot be applied to previously purchased exam or bundle. Details on how to access and download the coupon will be provided to registered attendees on November 17, 2020. For Attendee (Full Pass) Registrants:Register for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America Virtual to receive 50% off of the CKA or CKAD Exam. Visit The Linux Foundation training websites below to purchase your CKA or CKAD exam for $150 (typically $300). You may choose between the exam-only option listed at the top or a course + exam bundle listed in the Combine & Save section.30% off other courses or exams from LF Training! Visit The Linux Foundation training website and view the course catalog. This voucher may be applied towards any course or exam available for purchase in the catalog. Just add your coupon code during checkout to see your total discount. For Keynote Only Registrants:Register for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America Virtual to receive a flat $75 off coupon code good for any Linux Foundation Training product.
