						Attendee Gift					





All Full Pass attendees of KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America 2020 have the option to either receive an official Attendee Gift or forfeit the gift and donate the cost to the CNCF Diversity Scholarship Fund.  The Attendee Gift includes: 
Kubecon + CloudNativeCon branded socksCNCF Stickers IBM Stickers*HPE No Touch Tool*Red Hat RuMe Baggie All*Amazon Web Services RFID card holder for cell phones*
*Available to the first 5,000 attendees that request to receive the Attendee Gift. 
Attendees must complete the Attendee Gift request form by Friday, November 20, 2020, 7:00 PM EST in order to qualify.  A follow up email will be sent the week of November 29, 2020 with the instructions and the redemption code to receive their Attendee Gift.  
Once the attendee receives the redemption information, they have until Friday, December 18, 2020 at 11:59 PM EST to redeem.  Further instructions will be provided by email notification.
Limited to (1) Attendee Gift per attendee of KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America 2020. 
For questions, please email us at events@cncf.io
