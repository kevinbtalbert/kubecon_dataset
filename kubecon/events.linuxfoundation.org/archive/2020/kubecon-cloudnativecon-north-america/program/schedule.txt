All keynotes and sessions will be recorded. Session recordings will be available in the CNCF YouTube channel in December. 
The Sched app allows you to build your schedule but is not a substitute for your event registration. You must be registered for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America 2020 – Virtual to participate in the sessions. If you have not registered but would like to join us, please register here.
Please note: This schedule is automatically displayed in Eastern Time (UTC–05:00). To see the schedule in your preferred timezone, please select from the drop-down menu to the right, above “Filter by Date.”
