				Linux Kernel Maintainer Summit			









The Kernel Maintainer Summit will not take place virtually or in-person in 2020. 
However, the Kernel Summit track will continue to move forward as a part of Linux Plumbers Conference 2020. Kernel Summit is organized as a track at the Linux Plumber’s Conference (LPC) and is open to all registered attendees of LPC. To register, please visit the Linux Plumbers Conference website.
