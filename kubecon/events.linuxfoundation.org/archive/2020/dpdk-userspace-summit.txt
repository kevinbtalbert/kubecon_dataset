				DPDK Userspace Summit			




September 22 – 23, 2020 
Virtual Event#dpdkuserspace

DPDK Userspace Summit 2020 is a wrap! Thank you to all of the attendees, speakers, and our event sponsor, Arm, that joined us virtually this year. To experience the best of the event, be sure to watch all the sessions on the DPDK YouTube Channel, available by October 8. You can also review session slides from speakers who provided them via the event schedule.

The DPDK Userspace Summit 2020 is a community event designed for software developers who contribute to or use DPDK. The agenda for DPDK Userspace Summit 2020 will cover the latest developments and the roadmap suggestions for future releases. This is a unique opportunity to meet the community for discussing the challenges and innovations of the ecosystem.
