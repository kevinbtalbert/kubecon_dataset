						Register					





The Linux Foundation Member Legal Summit is an invitation-only event for member companies of The Linux Foundation. Invitation codes will be sent out directly to attendees for the event.
Registration details will be sent out shortly. Please email events@linuxfoundation.org with any questions.
Our registration system is now integrated with the Linux Foundation Single sign-on (SSO) platform. After clicking “Register Now”, you will be prompted to log in with your existing SSO account or to create a new one.
If you have forgotten the password for an existing account, reset it by clicking “Forgot Password.”
You will also be able to use 3rd party authentication providers (Gmail, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Facebook) to automatically generate your SSO Account.
If you experience issues, click the “Contact Us” link on the SSO page to connect with our support team.
Quick Note: We never sell attendee lists or contact information, nor do we authorize others to do so. If you receive an email claiming to sell an attendee list for a Linux Foundation event, please forward it to events@linuxfoundation.org.

CANCELLATIONS & SUBSTITUTIONS
So that we can better prepare for the event, if you are unable to attend, please log back into your registration using your confirmation number and select “unregister” or email us at events@linuxfoundation.org.
