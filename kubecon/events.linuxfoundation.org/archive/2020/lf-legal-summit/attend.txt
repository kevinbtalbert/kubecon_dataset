						About					





The Linux Foundation Legal Summit is a neutral legal forum where leading attorneys from companies leveraging open source solutions can work together to focus on and discuss issues of common interest and concern. The Linux Foundation Legal Summit invites counsel from member companies to attend and collaborate. The Legal Summit is open to the legal counsel of Platinum, Gold and Silver Members of The Linux Foundation.
