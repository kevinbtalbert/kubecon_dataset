						CNCF Project Office Hours					







Find below the schedule for our CNCF Project Office Hours during the KubeCon + CloudNativeCon EU Virtual Event. Our maintainers will be happy to answer questions and show demos. If you have any questions about the project office hours, please email events@cncf.io. Connect with maintainers on specific projects by visiting slack.cncf.io and search in the channel by #(project name). Please click on the project name in the schedule below to enter the Project Office Hour Zoom. If you have any problems with accessing the Zoom room, please reach out to #1-kubecon-eventhelpdesk on slack.cncf.io
