						Experiences					





The Open Networking & Edge Summit 2020 Virtual Experience offered a wealth of content with 80+ presentations from industry experts across the globe, plus unparalleled collaboration and experiences including:



Collaboration
After sessions conclude each day, attendees can join the nightly virtual collaboration opportunities to continue the conversation.LEARN MORE



Search + Connect
Collaborate with attendees across topic-specific chat rooms, search for attendees with common interests, and connect 1:1.



Fun + Games
Play games and trivia, earn badges and snap your photo in the virtual photo booth.








Ask the Expert Sessions
Engage directly with open networking and edge technical, business and community leaders daily in our Ask the Expert Sessions.
LEARN MORE



Wellness Experiences
Participate in wellness and zen activities – tai chi, yoga, desk stretching, puppy cams and more!
