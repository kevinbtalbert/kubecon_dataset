Automotive Grade Linux All Member Meeting Summer 2020 is now a virtual summit, happening July 15 – 16 due to COVID-19 safety concerns. All session times are in Central European Summer Time (CEST), UTC +2. 
REGISTRATION CLOSED


Our registration system is now integrated with the Linux Foundation Single sign-on (SSO) platform. After clicking “Register Now”, you will be prompted to log in with your existing SSO account or to create a new one.
If you have forgotten the password for an existing account, reset it by clicking “Forgot Password.”
You will also be able to use 3rd party authentication providers (Gmail, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Facebook) to automatically generate your SSO Account.
If you experience issues, click the “Contact Us” link on the SSO page to connect with our support team.

AGL’s All Member Meeting is an invitation-only event for member companies of The Automotive Grade Linux Foundation and invited guests. Invitation codes have been sent to member companies for the event.
Members: If you are a member company and need information regarding your invitation code, please contact events@automotivelinux.org.
Invited Guests: If you received an invitation to attend the event, register with the code.
