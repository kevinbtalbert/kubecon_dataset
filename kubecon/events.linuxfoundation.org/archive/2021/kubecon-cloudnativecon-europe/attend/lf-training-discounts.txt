						LF Training Discounts					





Linux Foundation Training is pleased to offer discounts for each attendee pass type for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2021 – Virtual. Each one-time use coupon is only available for attendees of the event and will expire as of 23:59 CEST on Monday, May 10, 2021. The discount cannot be combined with any other discount or promotion. Only valid for new training purchases and cannot be applied to a previously purchased exam or bundle. Details on how to access and download the coupon will be provided to registered attendees between Wednesday, May 5 and Friday, May 7, 2021. 
For All Access Registrants: Register for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe Virtual to receive 50% off (1) of the following: CKA, CKAD, CKS, CKA Bundle, CKAD Bundle, or CKS Bundle. Visit The Linux Foundation training website to purchase your CKA, CKAD, or CKS exam for $150 (typically $300). You may choose between the exam-only option listed at the top, or a course + exam bundle listed in the Bundle & Save section. 
For Keynote + Solutions Showcase Only Registrants: Register for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe Virtual to receive a flat $75 off one-time use coupon code good for any (1) Linux Foundation Training product.
